Your Growth Hacking Checklist

Over the next 100 days, we will be covering 3 main areas of the Growth Hacking Process. Acquisition, Optimisation and Scaling. To begin with, our sole focus will be to acquire as many users to your site as possible so we can begin to optimise your site and acquisition process, then scale the best performing methods to maximise your growth.

Perform the Quora Growth Hack

We want to perform Growth Hacks such as this as soon as possible, this isn't going to cause explosive Growth, but it will consistently deliver a steady stream of traffic and conversions to your site.

In a previous company I was receiving 2 $39 conversions every day from a single Quora answer, that's $28,000 per year and seeing as this was a recurring subsciption business with my customers average LTV at $400, it works out at the equivalent to $280,000+ in revenue from 1 Quora Answer!

Everytime you perform this Growth Hack, I suggest Answering 30-50 niche related questions, and you have to ensure your are delivering value in your answer. Don't just spam your website (quora users hate this). If you get upvoted on several high-traffic Quora questions, your are sitting on a goldmine of niche targeted website visitors.

Perform the Reddit Growth Hack

Once again, an absolute Goldmine for traffic. I've growh a business to six figures in revene from Reddit alone.

Similar approach to Quora, find reddit threads where users are asking for a tool similar to yours and promote your product or answer their question by saying your product can solve this issue for them.

Think of a good DJ on a night out, they don't spend the entire 1 hour set, but they will play a few of their songs and naturally mix it into their mix. In this case you need to be a content DJ, naturally mix links to your site into your content in a non-salesy way - it's a lot harder than it sounds :)

Try to get your product to the most upvoted answer and you'll see consistent streams of traffic and conversions.

TIP: After 6 months, Reddit posts are Archived and nobody is able to leave further comments, so try to have the most upvoted comment prior to the post turning 6 months old and your comment will remain #1 forver.

Create 5 YouTube Videos

These should be targeted to your niche and once again trying to help your target audience by delivering value to them. For example, I may create a video on how to create website conversions and promote Notifia in the process, but I'm not creating a sales pitch.

Nobody likes the feeling of being sold to, and if you can teach your users how you deliver value to them they are far more likely to convert.

Make sure your workflow doesn't result in your API keys being represented or stored in multiple places—this leads to bugs—or even ending up in your version control software.
