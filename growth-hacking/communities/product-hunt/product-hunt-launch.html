Product Hunt Marketing üê±

Product Hunt is a community of early adopters that enjoy using the latest tech/innovative products before everyone else. Very heavily poppulated with marketers, developers and entrepreneurs.

Do This 24/7

You can do this by following other users on Product Hunt, Commenting on Hunt's and just being an active member of the Product Hunt Community.
Add a link to your Product Hunt portfolio in the Bio section on Twitter, you could set up Twitter AF/UF bot to follow other users who tag #ProductHunt or similar.
Add link to your Product Hunt Portfolio in your LinkedIn bio and set up LinkedIn Helper to connect with other Entrepreneurs/users with the keyword "Product Hunt" on their profile.
Put Product Hunt link in your eMail signature. There's no official button at this point in time. But you could hire a designer to build you a button for about $10.
If you have employees/other team members, they should also aim to grow their Product Hunt accounts using the same methods above, this will help on the day you actuall launch.

Preparing for Launch

You can hire someone to do this on Fiverr, you'll get a decent Custom Explainer video for about $50-100. You can use this on Fiverr, Upload to YouTube, Wistia and also use for YouTube video adverts so it's a good investment.
This doesn't need to be a complex animation. The only reason we do this is because it's far more eye catching than a still image. If you Scroll through the Product Hunt Homepage you tend to find the highest upvoted products have animated images, even a simple Spin/Flash animation works well for this and you can gire an animator from Fiverr.
This goes without saying, you need a catchy and clear headline that explains your product well.
If you can, hire a designer/animator to make an Animated GIF of your app being used.
and of course, some screenshots exploring your app, the key tools/features and what you offer. I can't stress this enough, make sure they are high-quality and check the dimensions are correct when viewing them in Product Hunt. Nothing hurts a promotion more than crappy low-res images.

During Launch

  • Share link on Twitter
  • Share link on LinkedIn
  • eMail marketing campaign to all your users to let them know you're featured
  • Add welcome Message to all Product Hunt users on the day
  • Share all over Reddit
  • Share on Hacker News
  • Respond to all comments, try to get people talking about the product.

Product Hunt Growth Hack #2

Monitor Product Hunt Ask section for users searcing for apps similar to your own
