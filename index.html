Hello and welcome to Notifia Knowledge Base. My name's Nathan, I'm the Founder & CEO of Notifia. I'm 22 years old and based in London, UK.

I've worked in the digital space for 5 years, founding 8 succesful startups including one multi-million dollar business, I was also the most connected LinkedIn user in the UK for 2 year (58,000 connections) and I've solely grown a business from $0-79,000 MRR in under 100 days.

Whilst it hasn't all been a success, believe me when I say there have been plenty of failures. I've kept notes throughout my years of entrepreneurship, previously I kept these as internal docs I would use to teach new employees on how to succesfull grow a business. But I've realised just how much value this information can deliver, and have decided to publish them online, for free, for the world to enjoy.

The documents contain my notes and key takeaways from years of testing strategies, ideas not to mention countless hours of education, reading and failing.

I hope the information here serves you as well as it has me, the information here is also open for contribution (the source is available on GitHub.) All approved contributions will receive full credit in the article as well as a feature on our contribution page found here.
